Flexjet Scheduled Flights Tracker

This is a simple tool to help track scheduled and upcoming Flexjet flights to facilitate a more comfortable go-home day. You can search by a specific point or origin airport, or toggle the nearby filter to see what airports are within 50 miles of your location.

Keep in mind that this tool shows all scheduled Flexjet flights. There is no guarantee that a specific flight is an empty, non-revenue leg.

To ride company metal, the leg has to be empty and the crew has to have a 1.5 hour turn on point of arrival.

Once you identify a potential flight, call GCC and ask if it's empty and if you can tag along. Good luck!
